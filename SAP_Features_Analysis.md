### **Complement vs. Compete**
- **Don't try to replace SAP** - it's their established system
- **Complement SAP** - fill gaps, enhance workflows
- **Feed into SAP** - make your tools export-ready for SAP

### **Value Proposition**
- Your tools provide **structured, quantitative analysis** SAP may lack
- Your **position-specific metrics** add depth to SAP's general profiles
- Your **consistency scoring** provides unique insights
- Your **call capture** adds structured qualitative data

### **Best Approach**
1. **Understand their SAP workflow first**
2. **Identify gaps your tools can fill**
3. **Design for SAP compatibility from the start**
4. **Make integration seamless, not disruptive**
